## Biographical Information
Alexis Adams is a pioneer in deterministic AI systems and biomechanical engineering, with over 15 years of experience in regulated industries including healthcare diagnostics, financial compliance systems, and autonomous safety mechanisms. She holds a PhD in Computational Biomechanics from MIT (2010), where her doctoral thesis on "Verification Predicates in Stochastic Decision Systems" laid foundational work for deterministic envelopes, now implemented in Axiom Hive.
